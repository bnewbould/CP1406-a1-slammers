Beach Volleyball

Name: Benjamin Newbould (jc210391)

Goals

With this website, the goals are to generally increase awareness of Slammers Beach Volleyball and their services, to drive recruitment and to touch base with a new untapped demographic.

Success Evaluation

Success will be evaluated through the number of sign ups driven by the website. In order for this project to be a success, customer numbers must increase by at least 50%.

Target Audience

The target audience for this website is teenagers and young adults, ranging from age 15 to age 30. In order to reach this audience, the website will be designed with a contemporary look and feel, and certain phrasing will be chosen in order to appeal to the more competitive nature of this age bracket.
